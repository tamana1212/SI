About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Global Education and Opportunities!

At SI Overseas Consultancy, we are committed to turning dreams into realities. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to empower individuals with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to explore the world through education and career opportunities abroad. With a passionate team of experts and a wealth of experience, we have been dedicated to guiding students and professionals on their path to success since our inception.

Who We Are

We are more than just a consultancy; we are your partners, your advocates, and your mentors. Our diverse team of counselors, educators, and professionals come together with a shared passion for helping individuals reach their full potential. With a deep understanding of the challenges and possibilities that come with international pursuits, we provide a comprehensive range of services designed to make your journey seamless, informed, and rewarding.

Our Mission

Applying to international universities can be a daunting and complex process, but with our Application Assistance Service, we're here to make it smooth, efficient, and successful for you. Our team of experienced counsellors understands the intricacies of the application process and is dedicated to helping you present the best version of yourself to your dream universities.
